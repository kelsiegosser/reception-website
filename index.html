Details

Details

Join us in celebrating our marriage! The party is on Saturday, June 6, 2020. We will have an open bar with plenty of food to snack on throughout the night. This will be a fairly casual party - dancing is not required, although welcome. There is a game room, so bring your game face! We look forward to celebrating with all of you.

Wedding Celebration

Saturday, June 6, 2020
7:30 PM

Grand Rapids Brewing Company

Parking

Parking is available at several locations near Grand Rapids Brewing Co. You can find the best prices using BestParking or Parkopedia.

The closest parking ramp is going to be the City of Grand Rapids Ottawa-Fulton Parking Ramp which costs $1.25 per half hour or $12 for the night if there is an event. A map to this ramp is below.

Map

Guests

Feel free to bring a guest to our wedding celebration! Please include their name in the RSVP form below.

Kids

Unfortunately, kids are not able to attend our wedding celebration. The bar only permits 21 and over.

Food & Drink

We will have buffet style food, so please fill out the RSVP form along with any allergy or dietary restrictions so that we can accommodate you. We will have an open bar until 11:00 PM.

We have reserved a block of rooms at the Courtyard by Marriott Downtown for a discounted rate. You can book the room online or if you prefer to book via phone, call 877-901-6632 and use group name Stevenson-Gosser Wedding.

Flights

Grand Rapids, MI
GRR

Gerald R Ford International Airport (GRR) is where you should fly into. We like to use Google Flights to track flights and see prices from various airlines.

We recommend using Uber or Lyft rather than renting a car. It shouldn’t be difficult to find rides from the airport to downtown.
